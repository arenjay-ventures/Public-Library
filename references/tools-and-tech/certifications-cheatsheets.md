# Certifications Cheatsheets

## **Why Certifications Matter**

Professional certifications validate your skills and knowledge in specific technology areas. They provide:
- **Credibility**: Industry-recognized proof of competency
- **Career Advancement**: Higher salaries and better positions

- **Networking**: Access to professional communities
- **Continuous Learning**: Stay current with technology trends

## **CompTIA Certifications**

### **CompTIA A+ (220-1101 & 220-1102)**
**Entry-level IT certification covering hardware and software fundamentals.**

#### **What It Covers**
- **Hardware**: CPUs, RAM, storage, motherboards, power supplies
- **Operating Systems**: Windows, Linux, macOS installation and troubleshooting
- **Networking**: Basic network concepts, protocols, wireless technologies
- **Security**: Physical security, malware, authentication
- **Mobile Devices**: Smartphones, tablets, troubleshooting
- **Virtualization**: Hypervisors, VMs, cloud computing

#### **Study Resources**
- **Official Study Guide**: CompTIA A+ Complete Study Guide
- **Practice Tests**: CompTIA CertMaster Practice
- **Video Training**: Professor Messer (free), Mike Meyers (Udemy)
- **Hands-on Labs**: TryHackMe, Cybrary

#### **Exam Details**
- **Cost**: $246 per exam (2 exams required)
- **Duration**: 90 minutes each
- **Questions**: 90 multiple choice and performance-based
- **Passing Score**: 675/900 (220-1101), 700/900 (220-1102)
- **Validity**: 3 years

### **CompTIA Security+ (SY0-701)**
**Intermediate cybersecurity certification covering security fundamentals.**

#### **What It Covers**
- **Threats, Attacks & Vulnerabilities**: Malware, social engineering, attack vectors
- **Architecture & Design**: Security controls, secure network design
- **Implementation**: Identity management, PKI, cryptography
- **Operations & Incident Response**: Security monitoring, incident handling
- **Governance, Risk & Compliance**: Policies, risk management, compliance

#### **Study Resources**
- **Official Study Guide**: CompTIA Security+ Study Guide
- **Video Training**: Professor Messer (free), Jason Dion (Udemy)
- **Practice Labs**: TryHackMe, HackTheBox
- **Books**: "CompTIA Security+ All-in-One Exam Guide"

#### **Exam Details**
- **Cost**: $392
- **Duration**: 90 minutes
- **Questions**: 90 multiple choice and performance-based
- **Passing Score**: 750/900
- **Validity**: 3 years

### **CompTIA Network+ (N10-008)**
**Intermediate networking certification covering network technologies and troubleshooting.**

#### **What It Covers**
- **Networking Concepts**: OSI model, protocols, topologies
- **Infrastructure**: Cabling, wireless, cloud concepts
- **Network Operations**: Monitoring, optimization, troubleshooting
- **Network Security**: Threats, vulnerabilities, security controls
- **Network Troubleshooting**: Methodologies, tools, common issues

#### **Study Resources**
- **Official Study Guide**: CompTIA Network+ Study Guide
- **Video Training**: Professor Messer (free), Mike Meyers (Udemy)
- **Lab Practice**: Packet Tracer, GNS3, physical labs
- **Books**: "CompTIA Network+ All-in-One Exam Guide"

#### **Exam Details**
- **Cost**: $358
- **Duration**: 90 minutes
- **Questions**: 90 multiple choice and performance-based
- **Passing Score**: 720/900
- **Validity**: 3 years

### **CompTIA CySA+ (CS0-003)**
**Advanced cybersecurity certification focusing on security analytics and incident response.**

#### **What It Covers**
- **Security Operations**: Monitoring, analysis, threat hunting
- **Vulnerability Management**: Assessment, prioritization, remediation
- **Incident Response**: Detection, analysis, containment, recovery
- **Compliance & Assessment**: Risk assessment, compliance frameworks
- **Software & Systems Security**: Secure development, system hardening

#### **Study Resources**
- **Official Study Guide**: CompTIA CySA+ Study Guide
- **Video Training**: Jason Dion (Udemy), Cybrary
- **Hands-on Labs**: TryHackMe, HackTheBox, Blue Team Labs
- **Books**: "CompTIA CySA+ All-in-One Exam Guide"

#### **Exam Details**
- **Cost**: $392
- **Duration**: 165 minutes
- **Questions**: 85 multiple choice and performance-based
- **Passing Score**: 750/900
- **Validity**: 3 years

## **Study Strategies**

### **General Approach**
1. **Assessment**: Take practice tests to identify knowledge gaps
2. **Study Plan**: Create timeline with specific goals
3. **Hands-on Practice**: Use labs and real-world scenarios
4. **Review**: Regular review sessions and practice tests
5. **Exam Prep**: Final review and exam simulation

## **Maintenance and Renewal**

### **CompTIA Continuing Education**
- **CEUs**: Continuing Education Units required
- **Activities**: Training, conferences, teaching, additional certs
- **Cost**: $50 annual maintenance fee
- **Timeline**: 3-year renewal cycle

### **Google Certificates**
- **No Renewal**: Certificates don't expire
- **Updates**: Stay current with new course versions
- **Portfolio**: Build projects to demonstrate skills

### **WGU Degrees**
- **No Renewal**: Degrees are permanent
- **Continuing Education**: Pursue additional certifications
- **Alumni Network**: Access to professional development resources

Remember: **Certifications are stepping stones, not destinations**. Combine them with hands-on experience, projects, and continuous learning for maximum career impact.
